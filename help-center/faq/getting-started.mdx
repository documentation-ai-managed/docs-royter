## Overview

To get started with Royter:

1. Go to `https://app.royter.com`
2. Click "Sign Up" in the top right.
3. Enter your email and create a password.
4. Check your inbox for a verification email.
5. Click the link to activate your account.

Once activated, you can log in and begin using Royter. Explore features via the sidebar, and visit Settings to personalize your account.

If you have trouble logging in, try resetting your password from the login page.
