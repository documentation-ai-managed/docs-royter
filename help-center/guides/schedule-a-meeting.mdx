---
title: How to Schedule a Meeting in Royter
description: Schedule your first meeting using Royter in just a few steps.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Meetings">
    In the Royter sidebar, click on "Meetings".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'New Meeting'">
    Select "New Meeting" in the top right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set meeting details">
    Fill in the meeting title, date, time, and invitees.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send invites">
    Click "Create" to schedule the meeting and automatically send invites.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You're all set! Your invitees will receive notifications via email.
